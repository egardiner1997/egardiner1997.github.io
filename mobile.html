Week 2 Activity 1

Activity 01: Mobile Browsing

Facebook.com

Since facebook has gone to the new mobile design, I find it harder to find the things that I want. I preferred the way you had your groups and followed pages on the left side of screen in plain view and your games and contacts on the right side. Everything was easy to see and navigate. The only things that benefits from the new mobile view is the video viewing and timeline scrolling. Because you can choose between the videos easier and with timeline you basically scroll up and down to posts.

Instagram.com

Instagram itself is a website that benefits more from being mobile because you are scrolling through images and videos rather than clicking on multiple tabs. In the browser view, the screen is too big and there is too much space that needs filled up.

Fanfiction.net

Fanfiction.net is an online fanfiction site where writers and readers can readers can read and write alternate endings to the stories they have read. This site is also a place that I frequent when I don't want to read a normal book. I have a profile and prefer to use the mobile view rather than the browser view when I use the website. This is because I can navigate the categories and my profile with a simple click while in the browser view you have to scroll through a bunch of different menus to get what you need.
