Target Audience

Target Audience

The purpose of this website is to bring a weather report to the people in in a quick manner. Check Your Weather is a website that will give weather reports for 3 to 5 different cities/towns. The site is user friendly and will be available to anyone inside the community and outside the community (for visitors or tourists).

Personas

Persona Image Name Job Demographs Technology Motives
Female Persona Julianna Photographer Constantly traveling, always has her camera, single, associates degree, currently in an internship. Considers herself up to date with technology. Uses apps and websites like instagram and facebook to show off her work. Needs a quick and easy way to figure out when the best weather will be for images that she puts in her portfolio and on social media.
Male Persona Raphael Farmer New dad, loves to run, owns a few acres, grows tomatos and watermelons. Isn't used to a lot of technology, he knows the basics of how to search the internet and how to use an internet browser. Would like a way to figure out a way to check the weather so that he can take his new daughter around the property and to get exercise.

Scenarios

  1. What is the weather going to be in Christiansburg, today? I am thinking about going swimming.
  2. What is the chance of rain in Radford?
  3. I need to go for a run today, but the sky looks cloudy, is it going to rain?
  4. Its winter and I have an early class, what is the percent chance of snow?
  5. What is the current temperature right now?
