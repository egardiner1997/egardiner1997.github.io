Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to bring a weather report to the people in in a quick manner. Check Your Weather is a website that will give weather reports for 3 to 5 different cities/towns. The site is user friendly and will be available to anyone inside the community and outside the community (for visitors or tourists).
